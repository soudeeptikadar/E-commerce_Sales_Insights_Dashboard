## Key Points to be Addressed from the Data

This project aims to address the following key points through data analysis:

* **Monthly Sales Analysis:**
    * Calculate the monthly sales of the store.
    * Identify the month with the highest sales.
    * Identify the month with the lowest sales.

* **Sales Analysis by Product Category:**
    * Analyze sales based on product categories.
    * Determine the category with the highest sales.
    * Determine the category with the lowest sales.

* **Sales Analysis by Sub-Category:**
    * Perform sales analysis based on sub-categories.

* **Monthly Profit Analysis**
    * Determine which month had the highest profit.

* **Profit Analysis:**
    * Analyze profit by category.
    * Analyze profit by sub-category.

* **Customer Segment Analysis:**
    * Analyze sales and profit by customer segment.

* **Sales-to-Profit Ratio Analysis:**
    * Analyze the sales-to-profit ratio.
